1. The Prophet (pbuh) said :When one of you sneezes he should say, 
Alhamdhulillah

اَلْحَمْدُ للهِ

Meaning: 
Praise be to Allah 
2. Upon hearing this,his brother or companion should say to him, 
Yarhamukallah

يَرْحَمُكَ اللهُ

Meaning: 
May Allah bestow his Mercy on you 
3. Upon hearing this, one who sneezed shall say to him, 
Yahdheekumullahu wa yuslihu baalakum

يَهْدِيْكُمُ اللهُ وَيُصْلِحُ بَالَكُمْ

Meaning: 
May Allah give you guidance and improve your condition
(Book: Bukhari 6224)
