A’awudhu billahi minash shaithan 
أَعُوْذُ بِاللهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ

Meaning: 
Seek refuge with Allah from Satan. 
(Book: Bukhari 3282) 


OR 

A’awudhu billahi minash shaitha nir rajeem 
أَعُوْذُ بِاللهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيْمِ

Meaning: 
I Seek refuge with Allah from the accursed Satan. 
(Book: Bukhari 6115)
